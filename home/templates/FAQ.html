What is Hirecycle?

Hirecycle's goal is to make second-hand audio and video equipment accessible and affordable for everyone. We've created a community that shares, recyles and takes care of our environment. For more info, check out our about page.

How do I create a Hirecycle account?

If you don’t yet have an Hirecycle account you can go to our registration page. (Alternatively you can use the 'Register' button which can be found on the navigation bar at the top of this page). Enter your details and click ‘Create account’. Please note your username will be publicly displayed on the site.

How do I post a new ad?

You can post a new ad here. Fill out the form and click submit. Your ad will now appear in your own personal profile as well as the public all ads page

.
How do I edit an existing ad?

You can view your existing ads by navigating to your profile page. Find the ad you wish to edit and click on the edit button. This will open up a form to make your changes. Once you are happy, click submit and your changes will be saved. Your updated ad can now be viewed in your own personal profile as well as the public all ads page

.
I'm creating an advert but need some advice on how to set my rental rate. Can you help?

We'd be happy to help. Please drop Joe, our Customer Service Lead an email with your query and contact details. He'll be in touch and will help you decide on a rate.

I need some advice on insurance and payment options? Who should I contact?

Please drop Ella, our Finance Lead an email with your query and contact details. She'll be more than happy to help.

I need to report an ad or a user. How do I do this?

We're sorry to hear you've stumbled across an issue with an ad and/or a user. Please send us an email and we'll try to get this issue resolved as quickly as possible.

I'd like to work for Hirecycle. How do I apply?

Please send an email to our CEO Dave. Make sure to include your CV as well as any links to anything you think might be of interest (e.g. linkedin, twitter, github).

I have some feedback on the Hirecycle site and/ or service. Who do I contact?

Great news - we are always keen to hear feedback! Please send your feedback in an email to our CEO Dave
