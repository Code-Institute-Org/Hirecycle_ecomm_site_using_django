Where it all began...

A guy called Dave likes cameras. He meets a girl called Rachel who wants to shoot a documentary about climate change. Rachel wants to help the environment. Dave wants to help Rachel. So he lends her his trusty old JVC GR-C7 camcorder.

Where we are today...

Dave and Rachel are still friends and are firm believers in the fact that reducing, reusing and recycling electronics is one of the simplest and most effective ways to help our environment. This is why Dave founded Hirecycle - a community that, through recycling, makes second-hand audio and video equipment accessible and affordable for everyone.
